Empowering the Future: The Impact of The Young People’s Network International

At The Young People’s Network International in Kenya, we are dedicated to fostering the growth of young individuals who are shaping the future. Our initiatives empower youth to become leaders, innovators, and change-makers who contribute positively to society. Through mentorship, education, and community engagement, we are creating a lasting impact that resonates across Kenya.

Charlene Ruto

Founder, The Young People's Network International

Charlene Ruto is a passionate advocate for youth empowerment, leadership, and community development. As the founder of The Young People's Network International, she is dedicated to creating opportunities for young people to grow, connect, and impact the world positively.
